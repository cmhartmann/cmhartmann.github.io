About

Hi, I'm Cristina

I’m a writer who decided to give up a law career for the unexplored waters of writing. I string words together and hope for the best. Time will tell whether I’m an idiot or not.

I write science fiction books under the pen name Victoria Halley. I have published one book, The Secret Value of Zero, under this name. My next sci-fi book will be The Athenian Trials about world that has replaced bloody territorial wars with disputes resolved by gameplay.

For some samples and updates on my writing, check out: Stringing Words Together. To read my new fun side project, check out: How to Suck at Life.

I’m also planning to publish contemporary fiction under my real name. I’m currently working on a novel about a nerd turned pick-up artist called The Ladder. Also in the pipeline is a book about Internet trolls.

Follow me on Twitter (@cmmhartmann or on Facebook (Cristina Hartmann) if you want to keep abreast of my news and my 140-character ramblings.
